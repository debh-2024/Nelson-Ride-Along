Welcome to the Ride Along Club!

Want to go for a hackout with your horse but don't have anyone to go with?

Nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Nelson, New Zealand, our club invites you to bring your horse and experience a fun and relaxed horse ride with a group of new friends.

Join our community of passionate riders as we explore the stunning beauty of New Zealand's South Island. From tranquil forests to sandy beaches, our trails offer a diverse range of experiences for riders and horses.

To join, bring your own horse and immerse yourself in the beauty of Nelson's natural surroundings. Our club prioritizes safety and well-being, ensuring every rider feels confident and secure on their equine adventures. Embark on a journey of camaraderie and discovery with the Ride Along Club.

We meet every Saturday all throughout the year. The 1st and 3rd Saturday of each month, we meet at 8am. The 2nd and 4th Saturday of each month, we meet at 5pm. Contact us for the meet location.

Saddle up and explore the magic of group riding throughout Nelson, New Zealand!

About the Club

Founded in 2014, the Nelson Ride Along Club stands as a beacon of equestrian camaraderie and adventure.

It was founded by a group of likeminded equestrians keen to go on weekly adventures.

Every weekend, members converge upon the sprawling trails and verdant meadows that wind through the surrounding landscape. Mounted atop their trusty steeds, they embark on journeys that transcend the mundane and embrace the spirit of freedom. From novice riders seeking solace in nature to experienced equestrians craving the thrill of the open trail, each hack out promises an unforgettable experience.

We believe in the importance of companionship on the trails. Riders are encouraged to bring a fellow enthusiast to share in the adventure, ensuring safety and camaraderie along the way.

Beyond the thrill of the ride, the Nelson Ride Along Club fosters a sense of stewardship for both horse and habitat. Through responsible riding practices and environmental awareness, members champion the preservation of the natural beauty that surrounds them.

As the sun sets on another day of adventure, riders return to the stables with hearts full and spirits renewed, eagerly anticipating the next journey that awaits them along the winding trails of Nelson.
